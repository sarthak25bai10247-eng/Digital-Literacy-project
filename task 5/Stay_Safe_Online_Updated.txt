=====================================================================
              STAY SAFE ONLINE — CHECKLIST
=====================================================================


 1.  CREATE STRONG AND UNIQUE PASSWORDS
---------------------------------------------------------------------
     Use a combination of letters, numbers, and symbols for each
     password. Do not use the same password for more than one
     account.


 2.  ENABLE TWO-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION (2FA)
---------------------------------------------------------------------
     Activate 2FA on your email, banking, and social media accounts.
     This adds a second layer of protection beyond just your
     password.


 3.  DO NOT USE PUBLIC WI-FI FOR SENSITIVE TASKS
---------------------------------------------------------------------
     Avoid logging into banking or payment apps on public Wi-Fi
     networks. Use your mobile data or a trusted private network
     instead.


 4.  DO NOT CLICK ON SUSPICIOUS LINKS
---------------------------------------------------------------------
     If you receive an unexpected link via SMS, email, or WhatsApp,
     do not click it. Always confirm the identity of the sender
     before taking any action.


 5.  KEEP YOUR APPS AND DEVICES UP TO DATE
---------------------------------------------------------------------
     Install updates regularly. Updates contain important security
     fixes that protect your device from known threats.


 6.  NEVER SHARE YOUR OTP OR PERSONAL DETAILS
---------------------------------------------------------------------
     No bank or payment app will ever ask for your OTP, PIN, or
     password. If someone asks for these details, it is a scam.


 7.  ALWAYS VERIFY UPI PAYMENT REQUESTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------
     Before approving any UPI collect request, confirm the name of
     the recipient. Fraudsters frequently send fake payment requests
     to unsuspecting users.


 8.  ONLY SCAN QR CODES FROM TRUSTED SOURCES
---------------------------------------------------------------------
     Scanning a QR code can trigger a payment from your account.
     Never scan a code sent by an unknown person or source.


 9.  LOG OUT AFTER USING SHARED DEVICES
---------------------------------------------------------------------
     If you access your accounts on a public or shared computer,
     always log out completely when you are done.


10.  DOWNLOAD APPS FROM OFFICIAL STORES ONLY
---------------------------------------------------------------------
     Only install applications from the Google Play Store or Apple
     App Store to avoid downloading malware or fake apps.


=====================================================================
                  IN CASE OF ONLINE FRAUD
=====================================================================

  * Report immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal:
    https://cybercrime.gov.in

  * Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (available across India)

---------------------------------------------------------------------
  Following these steps will significantly reduce your risk and
  help you stay safe while studying, socialising, and managing
  finances online.
=====================================================================
